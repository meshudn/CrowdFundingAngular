Thank you so much for visiting Reuben's page.



I always struggled to watch adverts on TV about families being told their loved ones had cancer. Never really knowing how it must feel to hear those words; but now Thank you so much for your support.

I still find it hard to see Reuben's name on paperwork next to the words: stage 4, high risk, metastatic neuroblastoma.
